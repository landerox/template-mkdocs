# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

We expect all participants to:

- **Be respectful** – Treat all contributors with courtesy and respect
- **Be constructive** – Offer helpful feedback and engage in good-faith discussions
- **Be inclusive** – Welcome diverse perspectives and experiences
- **Focus on ideas** – Critique ideas, not people

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not acceptable:

- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusion of any kind
- Offensive or disrespectful language and personal attacks
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Spam, advertising, or low-effort contributions without genuine intent

## Enforcement

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening a private GitHub issue or contacting the repository maintainer directly.

All reports will be handled with discretion, and we'll respond promptly. We're committed to addressing concerns fairly and maintaining a respectful community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions within this repository, including pull requests, issues, discussions, and any communication related to this project.
